About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm Mitul Kumar Singh, a computer science student specializing in software development and machine learning. I have experience as a backend SDE intern at Oriserve and as a summer intern at Netaji Subhash University of Technology.
My skills include Python, C++, SQL, HTML, CSS, and frameworks like Jupyter Notebook and NodeJS. I've worked on projects such as an Intrusion Detection System, a Social Movie Diary, and a Musical Time Machine.
I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Jaypee Institute of Information Technology. Feel free to explore my achievements, certificates, and publications. If you have any inquiries or opportunities, please reach out via email by filling out form in bottom. Thank you for visiting!
